FAQ

Common questions

What is Bytly?

Bytly is a social restaurant app built around the people whose taste you trust: your friends. Discover spots on an interactive map, share honest reviews, and save places to custom wishlists.

How do I add friends?

Add friends by their username inside the app. Once connected, you'll see their reviews and saved spots right on your map.

Is Bytly free?

Yes — Bytly is free to use, and Sign in with Apple is supported. Your data is yours.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account anytime, right from Settings in the app. Deleting removes your account and associated data.
