Features

Everything you need to eat better.

Bytly is social at its core — add friends by username, follow their taste, and build a shared map of the best places to eat, wherever you go.

01

Recommendations from real friends

See your friends' reviews and saved spots right on the map. When someone whose taste you trust loves a place, you'll know instantly — no anonymous crowds, no noise.

👥Screenshot coming soonFriends' picks
02

Discover on the map

Explore restaurants around you with a fast, map-first interface. Friends' picks, your wishlists, and new discoveries each get their own markers, so you always know what you're looking at.

🗺️Screenshot coming soonInteractive map
03

Honest, detailed reviews

Leave quick reactions or full reviews with ratings, price, photos, and notes on the vibe, service, and standout dishes. Keep them friends-only or share them more widely.

⭐Screenshot coming soonWrite a review
04

Wishlists & collections

Save spots into custom collections like "Date Night," "Coffee Runs," or "Must-Try Ramen," all with your own icons. Your whole to-eat list, organized and always with you.

📚Screenshot coming soonYour collections
05

Powered by real place data

Search any restaurant and get accurate details like hours, location, photos, and cuisine — so your reviews and wishlists stay tidy and up to date.

🔎Screenshot coming soonSearch & details
06

Cuisine tags that get smarter

Tag spots by cuisine and vote on others' tags, so the right labels rise to the top and the map stays accurate for everyone.
